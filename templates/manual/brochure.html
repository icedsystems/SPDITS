Getting Started
1
Open ea.data.iced-eval.org in Chrome, Edge or Firefox.
2
Enter your email address and password → click Sign In.
3
Or click Sign in with Microsoft if your organisation uses Azure AD.
4
First login: you will be taken to a Set Your Password page before any other access.
5
Forgot your password? Click Forgot your password? on the login page and follow the email link.
💡 Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity for security. Save your work regularly.
Uploading Data (Partners)
1
Go to Uploads → click Upload File.
2
Select a CSV or XLSX file. Required columns: full_name, national_id, phone, county, gender, age.
3
Submit — the batch is queued for admin review. You will see the status update automatically.
📁 SFTP alternative: drop files in your designated SFTP folder — the system picks them up every 60 seconds.

Tracing & Interviews
Tracing Queue — Tracers & Supervisors
1
Go to Tracing in the sidebar. Use the partner filter to narrow the queue.
2
Click View Contacts to see decrypted phone / ID numbers. Data auto-hides after 60 seconds.
3
Update status: Located / Not Found / Deceased / Relocated. Add a note and save.
Assigning — Supervisors
1
Go to Assignments. Select traced participants from the list.
2
Choose an enumerator from your team → click Assign.
Recording Outcomes — Enumerators
1
Go to Interviews to see your assigned participants.
2
For each person record: Completed / Refused / Unreachable / Callback. Add notes and save.
✅ Interview outcomes are immediately visible to supervisors in real time.

Creating a User — Admin
1
User Management → Add User. Fill name, email, phone, role.
2
No password needed — user sets their own on first login.
3
To reset: click 🔑 next to the user → Force Reset.
Approving Uploads — Admin
1
Go to Uploads. Pending batches show a yellow badge.
2
Review → Approve. Participants are pseudocoded and direct identifiers are encrypted automatically.
🔐
Compliance & Audit
Re-identification — Compliance / Admin
1
Go to Compliance in the sidebar.
2
Search by pseudocode (e.g. PSN-2025-000123).
3
Enter a mandatory reason (min. 20 characters) and submit.
4
Direct identifiers (name, ID, phone) are shown for 60 seconds then auto-hidden.
⚠️ Every re-identification is permanently logged with your reason, IP address and session ID. This cannot be undone.
Audit Trail — Admin
1
Go to Audit. Every action in the system is recorded here — immutable and append-only.
2
Filter by user, action type or date range to investigate specific events.
Key Security Features
🔒Direct identifiers encrypted with Fernet AES
🆔Pseudocodes replace real names in all workflows
⏱️Auto session logout after 30 min inactivity
📋Immutable audit log for every action
👁️Contact data auto-hidden after 60 seconds
