ICED SPDITS — User Technical Manual

Secure Partner Data Intake, Tracing & Interview Management System
International Centre for Evaluation & Development
ea.data.iced-eval.org
Version 1.0 — May 2026
1. Accessing the System
1
Open ea.data.iced-eval.org in your browser.
2
Enter your email address and password, then click Sign In.
3
Alternatively click Sign in with Microsoft if your organisation uses Azure AD.
4
First login: you will be prompted to Set Your Password before accessing any page.
5
If you forget your password, click Forgot your password? on the login page and follow the email link.
Session expires after 30 min of inactivity — save your work regularly.
2. User Roles & Permissions
RoleKey Access
System AdminFull access — users, partners, uploads, all data, audit trail
Impl. PartnerUpload participant lists, view own submissions
SupervisorTracing queue, assign enumerators, create & view own enumerators
EnumeratorRecord interview outcomes for assigned participants
TracerTracing queue, update participant trace status, view contacts
ComplianceRe-identification with mandatory reason logging
3. Managing Users (Admin & Supervisor)
1
Go to User Management (sidebar). Supervisors see My Enumerators instead.
2
Click Add User / Add Enumerator. Fill in name, email and phone. Assign a primary role.
3
For supervisors with multiple partners, pick which partner the new enumerator belongs to.
4
Click Create User. No password is set — the user sets their own on first login.
5
To reset a password: click the 🔑 icon → Force Reset. User will be prompted on next login.
6
To assign a supervisor to multiple partners: edit the user → tick partners under Additional Partners.
4. Managing Partners (Admin only)
1
Go to Partners (sidebar) → click Add Partner.
2
Fill in the partner's name, unique code, contact details, and optional SFTP username / directory.
3
Save. The partner code is used as the upload folder name on the server.
5. Invitations (Admin only)
1
Go to Invitations → New Invitation. Enter the recipient's email and role.
2
An email with a secure link is sent. The recipient clicks it to activate their account.
6. Uploading Participant Lists (Partner)
1
From the dashboard click Upload File or go to Uploads.
2
Select a CSV or XLSX file. Required columns: full_name, national_id, phone, county, gender, age.
3
Submit. The file is validated and queued. You will see the batch status update automatically.
4
SFTP alternative: drop files into your designated SFTP folder — the system polls every 60 seconds.
Files are stored under /media/uploads/<PARTNER_CODE>/ on the server.
7. Approving & Processing Uploads (Admin)
1
Go to Uploads. Pending batches show a yellow Pending badge.
2
Review the batch details. Click Approve to proceed or Reject with a reason.
3
On approval, participants are pseudocoded (PSN-YYYY-NNNNNN) and direct identifiers are encrypted automatically.
4
Duplicates are flagged and merged; quasi-identifiers (county, gender, age) remain visible.
8. Tracing Participants (Tracer / Supervisor)
1
Go to Tracing. Use the partner filter to narrow the queue.
2
Click View Contacts to see decrypted phone/ID numbers (auto-hidden after 60 seconds).
3
Update the participant's trace status: Located / Not Found / Deceased / Relocated.
4
Add a note and save. The status change is logged in the audit trail.
9. Assigning & Interviewing (Supervisor / Enumerator)
1
Supervisor — Assignments: Go to Assignments, select traced participants, choose an enumerator, click Assign.
2
Enumerator — Interviews: Go to Interviews to see your assigned list.
3
For each participant record the outcome: Completed / Refused / Unreachable / Callback.
4
Add any relevant notes and save. The interview status is immediately visible to supervisors.
10. Re-identification & Compliance (Compliance / Admin)
1
Go to Compliance in the sidebar.
2
Search for the participant by pseudocode (PSN-YYYY-NNNNNN).
3
Enter a mandatory reason for re-identification (minimum 20 characters).
4
Direct identifiers are displayed for 60 seconds then automatically hidden.
Every re-identification access is logged with reason, IP address and session ID — it cannot be undone from the audit trail.
11. Audit Trail & Notifications (Admin)
1
Go to Audit in the sidebar. Every action in the system is recorded here — immutable, append-only.
2
Filter by user, action type, or date range to investigate specific events.
3
Notifications: click the 🔔 bell icon (top right) to view in-app alerts. Click Mark all read to clear.
4
Email alerts for key events are configured under Email Settings (sidebar → Admin section).
